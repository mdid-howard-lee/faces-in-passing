About the Game

Every connection is calculated. Every choice matters.

IMG: CLIMBING THE HIERARCHY

Purpose: Climb or Fall

FACES IN PASSING is a tense mobile roleplay simulation about striving to be prom queen at Rosewood High. Play as Astrid Cole, a former student whose popularity is failing. Risk it all and do anything necessary to become the most popular character, clawing your way back to the top by navigating a landscape of shifting alliances, fractured trust, and social manipulation.

Game Location: Rosewood High School

Astrid Cole

She’s no longer as liked and popular as before, now choosing between staying “Who I Am?” or seeking to be “Who They Want.” Every hallway interaction, social media post, and private conversation forces her to decide: true identity, or a temporary, calculated mask?

Game Mechanics

Intimate first-person perspective, expansive social control.

Game Setup

FACES IN PASSING is a mobile game, adopting an intimate first-person point of view (POV). Navigate the crowded corridors and specific locations of Rosewood High using an intuitive mobile joystick, monitor conversation loops, and interact with students based on their unique behavior patterns and social statuses.

The core gameplay dynamic is defined by choice: Your choice in conversation can change the plot and lead to different consequences. Using a dedicated mobile dashboard, review conversation history, manage likes and chats, and track the consequences of your social manipulations across Rosewood High’s hierarchy.
